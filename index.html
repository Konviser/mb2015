what is webcamp

WebCamp Ljubljana is the unconference where Web developers share knowledge, experience, and vim configurations.

It's organized by the community for the community. There will be over 300 attendees from Slovenia and the region. All talks will be in English.

There will be three tracks: Front-end, Back-end and DevOps. And the fourth, most important one: Good Time with old and new friends, good coffee and lots and lots of discussions with interesting people.

location

We thank the Faculty of Computer and Information Science in Ljubljana for hosting us. The Faculty is a leading research and teaching institution for information sciences with several active research groups and distinguished alumni.

call for proposals

Come give us a talk on:

  • Front-end (HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, ..)
  • Back-end (Frameworks, Languages, Middleware)
  • DevOps (Virtualization, Orchestration, Practical approaches)
  • or anything web developers care about

Talk format:

  • You have 25 minutes. Your talk should work offline. Screenshots are your best friends.
  • We want interesting stories. Everyone can read a tutorial.
  • We like videos more than demos. Come prepared.
  • You can suggest a 5 minute lightning talk at the venue.

Timeline:

  • Friday, February 13th - Submission deadline
  • Friday, February 20th - We notify The Chosen Ones
  • Monday, February 23rd - We tell everybody
