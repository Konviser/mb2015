what is webcamp

BarCamp is an ad-hoc gathering born from the desire for people to share and learn in an open environment.
It is an intense event with discussions, demos and interaction from participants who are the main actors of the event. (from barcamp.org).

WebCamp.si is a sub-genre of the BarCamp that is oriented at the Slovenian Web professional community.
